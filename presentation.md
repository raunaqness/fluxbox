# <span class="app-name">FluxBox</span>

**A macOS menu bar app that replaces 6 open tabs.**

<br>

One click gives you: live exchange rates, stock & crypto prices, a unit converter, world clocks, recent files, system stats — and your Claude API usage.

Everything is drag-and-droppable. Hide what you don't need.
